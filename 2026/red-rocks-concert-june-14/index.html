      Worth knowing

      Parking is free but first-come — the upper south lot is the shortest walk, and the whole place empties at once, so a slow exit beats circling. Mountain weather flips in minutes: a sunny 75° load-in can become a 50° drizzle by the encore. One warm layer and a packable rain shell turn a miserable night into a great one.
